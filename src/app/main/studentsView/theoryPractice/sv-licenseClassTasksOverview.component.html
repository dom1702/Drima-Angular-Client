Welcome to theory exam pratice for class {{currentLicenseClass.token}}!
This test consists class questions (10 pcs), risk identifying questions (5 pcs) and traffic situation questions (50 pcs).
Traffic situation questions have ten different serie which each have five animated situation picture. These questions are answered by using buttons Yes and No.
It is also possible to Mark a question and return to it after all the questions. There is totally 30 minutes to do the test. After that all the questions not answered are marked as incorrect.
After the test you will get a result and feedback. The test will be acknowledged, if there are max. 4 incorrect answers in class questions, max 1 in risk identifying questions and max. 7 incorrect answers in traffic situation questions.
